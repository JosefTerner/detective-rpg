# Detective RPG — Game Rules

> Card-based role-playing game for 4–20 players, set in a small town (population 5,000).

---

## Game Overview

The detective's goal is to solve a crime by investigating evidence, interrogating witnesses, and using strategic deduction. Other players either assist in the investigation or attempt to mislead it.

---

## Game Setup

- **Minimum players:** 4 (some players may hold multiple role cards)
- **Maximum players:** 20
- Pre-created crime scenarios define only the crime location — details must be uncovered through investigation.
- Each player receives a role card with character guidelines, including alibis and motivations.
- The detective and their partner (if present) hold only one card each.
- The detective starts at the police department.

---

## Roles & Responsibilities

### Detective & Partner Detective (if applicable)

- The detective is the only player responsible for solving the crime.
- The partner detective assists but does not hold multiple cards.
- They gather evidence, verify alibis, and interrogate suspects.
- They can request hints via anonymous tips (which may be true or misleading).
- They can delegate police officers to bring in suspects for interrogation.

### Supporting Roles (Always Tell the Truth)

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| **Policeman** | Guards the crime scene, provides initial information, transports suspects |
| **Coroner** | Provides cause of death and additional insights upon request |
| **Witnesses** | Provide truthful accounts of events |

### Opposing Roles (May Lie)

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| **Killer** | Tries to avoid detection; may provide false evidence |
| **Accomplices** | Help mislead the detective; cannot manipulate physical evidence |
| **Town Residents** | Provide neutral or misleading information based on their role cards |

---

## Game Flow — Investigation Steps

1. **Arrive at the Crime Scene**
2. **Gather Initial Information** — question the police officer about the scene
3. **Consult the Coroner** — ask for cause and time of death
4. **Search for Evidence** — examine objects, documents, and clues
5. **Interview Witnesses** — gather testimonies from nearby people
6. **Identify Suspects** — determine who had motive and opportunity
7. **Interrogate Suspects** — structured questioning to test alibis
8. **Verify Testimonies** — check footage, call workplaces, visit locations
9. **Reconstruct the Timeline** — piece together the event sequence
10. **Final Review** — reanalyse findings with the partner detective (if present)
11. **Make a Verdict** — accuse the killer (false accusations cost points)

---

## Time Mechanics & Travel Rules

Each investigative step costs in-game time. Travel time varies by distance and peak traffic:

| Time Window | Effect |
|-------------|--------|
| Peak hours (7–9 AM, 1–2 PM, 6–7 PM) | +15 minutes to all travel times |
| Off-peak | Standard travel time |

### Police Officer Deployment

- The detective has **two police officer cards** to delegate tasks.
- Officers transport suspects to the police department; travel is double the one-way time.
  - Example: suspect 10 minutes away → 20 minutes to arrive.
- Officers are unavailable while on a task and must return before reuse.

### On-Site Interrogations

- The detective can visit suspects at their workplace or home.
- Must be accompanied by a partner or one officer.
- Multiple suspects at the same address can be interrogated together; separate addresses require separate trips.

---

## Verification Tools

The detective can verify alibis at any time:

- Call workplaces for confirmation
- Check security footage at shops or businesses
- Consult additional witnesses
- Use free officers to assist

---

## Hints & Anonymous Tips

- The detective can draw a **hint card** at any step.
- Hints may be true or misleading.
- No limit on how often hints can be drawn.

---

## Scoring System

| Factor | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Time taken | Faster resolution = more points |
| Correct deductions | Points awarded for identifying the killer |
| False leads followed | Reduces score |
| Wrong accusations | Costs points |
| Solved within 3 in-game days | Bonus; delayed solutions score less |

---

## Winning the Game

| Player | Win condition |
|--------|--------------|
| Detective | Correctly identifies the killer |
| Killer | Detective fails to solve the case or accuses an innocent person |
| Accomplices | Successfully mislead the detective |
| Witnesses / Police / Coroner | Case is solved correctly |
